The department of computer science at the Nasarawa state university, Keffi strives to give the students a high-quality education in the computer studies. The department offers B.Sc. program in computer science. The department serves the needs of student throughout Nigeria by offering program that prepare them with applied knowledge and practical skills in the mathematical sciences. It also promotes critical thinking; creative problem solving and helps them develop effective communication and entrepreneurship skills. As part of the mission, the lecturers engage in applied integrative, collaborative and pedagogical scholarship. Computer science deals with the fundamental scientific laws and engineering principles which govern the design, implementation, use and maintenance of computer system. A computer scientist is involved in work ranging from mathematical and empirical studies of problem-solving procedures of research in advanced computer systems. The field covers the study of theoretical and applied principles of computer system. As computer systems have become a part of everyday life, the demand for specialized professionals in the area has increased astronomically. The B.Sc. degree program in computer science is designed with core courses that provide breadth and depth in the field along with a strong theoretical component as a foundation for good software engineering and information systems. The program includes courses in chemistry, physics and mathematics, general education requirements in the humanities and in-depth sequence of specialized computer science courses. Core courses for our B.Sc. program in computer science include principles of programming, data structures and algorithms, computer architecture, operating systems, data communication and database systems supported by a strong foundation in computing principle such as Design and implementation. The program provides a balance on theoretical core courses covering both software and hardware through integrated lectures, laboratory sequences and individual and group projects often with direct application.

The department of computer science, Nasarawa State University, Keffi has set for itself the following objectives:

  1. To create in students the awareness of and enthusiasm for computer science and its capabilities.
  2. To involve the students in an intellectually and stimulating and satisfying experience of learning ad studying.
  3. To provide a broad and balanced foundation in computer science knowledge and practical skills.
  4. To accomplish outstanding research in computer science and their application which are fundamental and useful to innovative scientific advancement.
  5. To provide an excellent education in computer science for undergraduate students that is of the highest international standards.
  6. To recognize and respond to the changing needs of the society through specific high-quality research, teaching programs and initiatives.
  7. To develop in students through an education in computer science, a range of transferable skills of value in information technology to all aspect of human endeavor.
  8. To generate in students an appreciation of the importance of computer science in an industrial economic, environment and social context.
  9. o provide students with knowledge and skills base from which they can proceed to further studies in computer science or multi-disciplinary areas involving computer science.
  10. To provide students with knowledge and skills needed for industrial, entrepreneurship and self-employment.

To develop exceptional research competence and build extreme human ability via research, training and innovation activities for the information science and technology in Nigeria.

To be a global concentration of research and teaching in computer science.
