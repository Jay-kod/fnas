BRIEF HISTORY

The Faculty is one of the Foundation Faculties of Nasarawa State University, Keffi (NSUK) established in October 2001 alongside the inception of the University. At inception the Faculty started with four academic departments, namely: Biological Sciences, Chemistry, Mathematical Sciences and Physics. In October 2002, the Department of Geography as well as Geology & Mining came on-board. The Department of Biochemistry & Molecular Biology was established in 2006. The first set of students in the Faculty graduated in the 2004/2005 session. The Department of Biological Sciences has since metamorphosed into departments of Microbiology, Plant Science & Biotechnology as well as Zoology, while the Mathematical Sciences has metamorphosed into Computer Science, Mathematics and Statistics Departments. The Department of Science Laboratory Technology was the last established in 2014, while the Department of Geography was transferred to the Faculty of Environmental Science. Department of Biochemistry and Molecular Biology was re-named Biochemistry Department in 2019.

Benefits: Why Choose NSUK/FNAS?

The Nasarawa State University, Keffi is a top choice school for students seeking quality education. It attracts over 35k+ students because of it’s innovative technology, depth of staff expertise and capacity to help students reach their full potential.

The university has excellent students,the likes of Abdullazeez Isa, who took part in just concluded Huawei Intenational Competition and Emerged as the champion. and Dama Micheal, who was among the just concluded 3MTT hackanton and came out as the first runner. It has proved over time to produce industry ready graduates who are preferred choices for employers.

Faculty Life: What To Expect

The first year in the University presents a lot of new experiences: living away from home, meeting new people and managing your own time.

Prepare for life on faculty by figuring out what to bring, how to handle challenges.
